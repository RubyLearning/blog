  </h3>
  
  <p class="update">
    RubyLearning is pleased to announce the promotion of the book &#8220;<strong><a href="http://www.manning.com/fogus/">The Joy of Clojure</a></strong>&#8221; by author Michael Fogus and Chris Houser.
